A Fact About Teddy Bears

After the Titanic sank in 1912, German toy company Steiff created 500 teddy bears to honor the victims. The “mourning bears” were black with red-rimmed eyes to show their sympathy. They now sell for $20,000 or more in auctions. "rd.com"

Crane

A Fact About Cranes

The traditional paper crane is probably the most famous of all origami models. It’s designed after the Japanese red-crowned crane. The Japanese name for this model is “Orizuru” which simply means “Folded crane.” “Ori” is the same “Ori” that you find in the word origami.

Puppy

A Fact About Puppies

Puppies can be born green. It seems impossible, but there have been some recorded cases in which the colour isn’t permanent and fades after a few weeks

Chicken

A Fact About Chickens

Chickens can dream! Chickens experience rapid eye movement (REM) during sleep, which means they dream just like we do.

Crabs

A Fact About Crabs

The lifespan of a small crab averages around 3-4 years, but larger species such as the giant Japanese spider crab can live as long as 100 years. Looks like the turtles have some healthy competition!

Apatosaurus

A Fact About the Apatosaurus

The Apatosaurus is one of the largest animals to ever live on earth. It was 75 feet long and 50,000 pounds!
